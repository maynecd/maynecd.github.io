Gamifying Your Classroom

COMPETENCY: Leadership & Social Responsibility: To provide students with experiences to become socially responsible, reflective/sensitive on the interaction of technologies & society and act ethically in response to current and future challenges of emerging technologies for learning.

Course: IS_LT 9485 Designing Online Learning

Project Description: This group project resulted in the development of an online instructional module that provided learners with the knowledge, skills, and appropriate abilities to show an understanding of game-based learning principles and gamification. The online course module teaches learners how to define and identify elements of game-based learning/gamification and how to apply those concepts to the creation of an online lesson using GooseChase.

Artifacts:

Gamifying Your Classroom

The project team consisted of four group members who all had some type of role in education of varying ages/environments. The link provided shows the final prototype for the online instructional module. I served as the project lead and oversaw the project deliverables and timelines. This project represents the competency above through the representation of the objective listed above.

Reflection: This project went very well and the background of each member was a definite strength in addition to all of us having experience with gamification and integrating game elements into meaningful online learning. One of the biggest weaknesses of the project was the ambiguity in the project guidelines. The expectations were unclear throughout the process. I learned a significant amount of information on the characteristics of meaningful online learning and the framework to designing meaningful learning experiences. Our project made use of the GooseChase platform, Google sites, concept mapping, and collaborative workspaces for our project design.
