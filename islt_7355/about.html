About Me

A Little About Me:

Hi! My name is Christina and I am from Kansas City, Missouri. I work at an academic hospital where I serve as the Emergency Medicine residency program manager. I am currently a graduate student in the Master of Science Learning Technologies & Design degree program at the University of Missouri-Columbia. My anticipated graduation date is May of 2023.

In May 2016, I completed the Bachelor of Health Sciences degree at the University of Missouri-Columbia. I chose to pursue the graduate degree program to better prepare myself to take on new opportunities within my career field and support my residency program’s commitment to leading-edge academic medicine and providing our residents outstanding, unparalleled educational opportunities.
